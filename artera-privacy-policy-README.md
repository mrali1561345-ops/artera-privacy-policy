# Privacy Policy for Artera: Art & Quote Journal

Last updated: July 2026

Artera ("the Application") is built as a commercial offline utility app. This service is provided at no cost and is intended for use as-is.

## 1. Information Collection and Use
Artera values your privacy. The Application does NOT collect, stream, or transmit any personally identifiable information, location data, or analytical data to external servers. 

## 2. Data Storage
All data, including your typed quotes, selected icons, and monthly preferences, are stored locally on your device via secure local cache methods (SharedPreferences). No data is synchronized with third-party cloud architectures or networks. Clearing your application cache or uninstalling the app will permanently remove this data.

## 3. Third-Party Access
The Application does not interface with any third-party APIs, advertising networks, or user tracking SDKs. 

## 4. Children’s Privacy
Since the Application does not gather any data whatsoever, it is completely safe for audiences of all ages, including children under the age of 13.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

## 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions regarding this privacy framework, feel free to contact us via our developer channel.
